# CT Drive

CT Drive is a car rental company based in Mombasa, Kenya. We offer a wide range of cars for rent, including sedans, SUVs, vans, minibuses, and luxury cars. Our cars are available for rent in Mombasa, Nairobi, Diani, Malindi, and other locations in Kenya. We also offer airport transfers and chauffeur-driven cars. Our cars are equipped with the latest technology and safety features to ensure a safe and comfortable journey for our customers. We are committed to providing the best service to our customers and ensuring their satisfaction. Thank you for choosing CT Drive.
